Who we are

Built by someone who gets it.

Láimhe was built out of frustration. Watching skilled tradespeople — electricians, plumbers, tilers, carpenters — lose jobs and waste hours every week not because of their work, but because of the admin chaos around it.

We're an Irish team that believes the best tradespeople in the country deserve tools as good as their craft. Láimhe is simple by design — because you shouldn't need a manual to run your own business.

— Based in Ireland. Building for Ireland.
